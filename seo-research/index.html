Search engines

How does a search engine work?
A search engine tries to match the users search with the best possible websites. It does this by searching (or crawling) where it looks through the code and the content of sites to find relevant text and images. The search engine will index this information for future searches. After finding websites, the search engine will rank those sites based on what it believes to be the most relevant to the searcher.
Why do different search-engines give different ranking to websites for the same search phrase?
Search engines have different algorithms which put a different amount of weight on each factor that they consider. Google for example puts much less emphasis on metadata, while other major search engines (Bing, Yahoo, and Baido) do include metadata as part of their search ranking. The same goes for other factors such as the age of the domain name and the quality and quantity of backlinks.
What is a <meta /> tag?
Meta tags are code that helps search engines figure out what the content of a particular site is.
What is the purpose for Keyword and Description meta tags?
The keyword meta tag is a list of just that, keywords, which help tell the search engine the relevant or important parts of your pages. The description meta tag is just a brief description of your page and its content.
Why might you include meta tags in your pages?
Meta tags will help with some search engine rankings and are used to display relevant text to searchers.
Do meta tags make your pages more easily searchable or improve its ranking?
With Google being the search engine that over 90% of searches are made on and the fact that they do not put emphasis on meta tags (some sources even say too many will hurt your ranking) you will want to be careful with which meta tags you use. Doing this will optimize your rankings on other search engines such as bing or yahoo, while not affecting your google ranking.
What are several ways that you can publicize your personal website project and make it more visible on the web?
To make my website more visible on the web, I would want to include keyword and description metatags. I would also want my pages to have clear and concise content that would show up well on a search. Lastly, any pages that did not have a ton of relevant information on them, could be given the noindex attribute, which would prevent them from being indexed and possibly increase the sites ranking.
