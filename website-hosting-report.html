What is a web server? Why would you need one?

A web server stores the files that make up a website and they deliver those webpages to people visiting your website. You would need a web server for your site to exist on the internet.

Why is getting a domain name is not enough to put your site up on the web?

A domain name is not enough to put your site up on the web because you would have no way of receiving or transmitting information to the user.

What service does a website hosting company provide?

A website hosting company leases web server space to their clients. This is important because web servers are very expensive and probably unaffordable for most businesses.

What are the most important characteristics to look for in selecting a good web hosting company?

Important characteristics to look for in a web hosting company are:

  • Reliability - Making sure the site stays up.
  • Speed - Slow websites will drive away users.
  • Bandwidth - Similar to speed, this is how many users you can support at once. You want to be able to support many customers at your peak times.
  • Space - Most websites will probably want room to grow.
  • Support - So you can get help when you need it.

What is average price for hosting a website?

For regular website hosting plans, they seem to average around three to five dollars per month, with many having premium plans that are 3-5 times more expensive.

What are the most important features for you when reviewing a hosting plan?

  • Reliability/Site Uptime
  • Space for your website and for user traffic (Space and bandwidth)
  • Site speed
  • Support
  • Pre-installed apps - These include apps for website creating and management and ecommerce tools such as a shopping cart. These seem less important for more skilled web developers.
