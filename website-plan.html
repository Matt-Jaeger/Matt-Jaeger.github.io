Website Plan

Website Plan

Website topic and title

  • The topic of my website is going to be a board game store that serves food and drink
  • The title for the webpage will be the name of the game store

Target Audience

  • People of all ages and genders
  • Board/tabletop game enthusiasts
  • Card game enthusiasts (TCG/CCG)
  • Anyone who wants to go out with friends or family to play a game and have a meal or a couple drinks

Site Purpose

The site purpose will mainly be to advertise the store. It will display pictures of the store, opening and closing times, show any events or specials that are coming up, and have shop sections for board games, card games, and trading card games.

Similar Websites

  • Mox Boarding House
  • Zulu's Board Game Cafe
  • Game Hause Cafe

Website Features

  • Calender
  • Photos
  • Contact Form
  • Shop Page
  • Upcoming Events

Website Personality

  • Warm and welcoming
  • Minimal words
  • Lots of pictures
  • Community minded

Website Limitations

  • Expect people to have decent internet connections, there will be quite a few pictures.
  • Expect people to have some comfort with techonology
  • Would expect users to visit on mobile devices
  • Would expect the full spectrum of browsers, chrome, firefox, ie, safari, opera, etc
